Meet SafetyPin...
your personal Safety Companion.
SafetyPin is a real-time mapping app designed to keep you informed and protected. Whether it’s reporting crimes, tracking lost items, or checking the safety of specific areas, SafetyPin provides centralized, community-driven updates to help you avoid high-risk zones. Say goodbye to scattered social media posts and unreliable broadcasts— SafetyPin makes traveling safe simple, intuitive, and reliable.
We’re here to help you.
Stay Alert.
Get real-time updates on nearby incidents so you can make informed decisions, whether you're commuting, traveling, or out alone.
Stay Connected.
Join a community that looks out for each other by sharing reports and updates that help everyone stay safe.
Stay Aware.
View risk levels in different areas at a glance, helping you navigate safely—whether at home or in a city you've never been to before.

Pricing
SafetyPin is a location-based social media platform that connects people based on shared safety concerns, interests, and local experiences. We offer two account types to support your journey: Free and Premium.
Free Plan
Rp0/month
Perfect for everyday users who want to stay updated and connected.
  • Browse public posts
  • Upload up to 3 posts per day
  • Upload posts with title at maximum 70 characters
  • Upload posts with description at maximum 200 characters
Premium Plan
Rp20k/month
  • Post up to 10 per day
  • Upload posts with title at maximum 140 characters
  • Upload posts with description at maximum 800 characters
