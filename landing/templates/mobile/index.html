Meet SafetyPin...
your personal Safety Companion.
SafetyPin is a real-time mapping app designed to keep you informed and protected. Whether it’s reporting crimes, tracking lost items, or checking the safety of specific areas, SafetyPin provides centralized, community-driven updates to help you avoid high-risk zones. Say goodbye to scattered social media posts and unreliable broadcasts— SafetyPin makes traveling safe simple, intuitive, and reliable.
